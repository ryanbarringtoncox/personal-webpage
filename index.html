I got my first cassette 4-track recorder in high school and gradually become a recording junkie. I like natural sounds, roomi-ness, tape. “A Hard Day’s Night” sounds higher-fi-er to me than all the new music I’ve heard. I’m working on a follow-up album to self-titled. Fellow hackers can see some of my code.
